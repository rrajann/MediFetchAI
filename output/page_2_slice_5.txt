Diagnostic Tests
●
Blood tests including Complete Blood Count, Comprehensive Metabolic Panel.
●
MRI of the brain to rule out any neurological cause for headaches.
●
ECG to assess cardiac function given history of palpitations.
Assessment and Plan
●
Differential diagnosis includes tension headaches, migraine, hypertension-related
issues.
●
Plan to adjust hypertension medication and monitor blood pressure.
●
Follow up after MRI results and blood test results.
